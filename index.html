How to Play GCBC

The Goal

Have the lowest total sum of cards in your hand when the game ends! The winner is the player with the smallest score.

How to Finish

You start with 3 Bad Cards. You become "Finished" once you successfully turn all 3 into Good Cards. Once everyone is finished, scores are compared.

Visibility

You see your opponents' Bad Cards, but they are hidden from you. You see your own Good Cards, but opponents can't. Turn Bad cards into Good cards to hide their value!

Moves

  • Move 1 (Swap): Replace 1 of your Bad Cards with the center Trade card. That card becomes a Good Card.
  • Move 2 (Take): Sacrifice (Trash) 1 of your Good Cards to draw a random card from the deck. This new card is a Bad Card. (Use this to get rid of a high-value card!)
  • Move 2 (Trade): Sacrifice (Trash) 1 of your Good Cards. Steal a Bad Card from an opponent (it becomes your Good Card). The opponent then steals a Bad Card from you (it becomes their Good Card). Finally, you draw a new Bad Card from the deck.
